Introduction

Introduction Smart Street light systems using IoT tech nology are the next generation of street lighting systems that offer improved energy efficiency, reduced costs, and better control and management of street lighting. The basic concept of a smart street light system is to incorporate sensors, wireless communication, and intelligent controllers into the street light infrastructure. These smart lights can be programmed to automatically turn on and off based on the surrounding light levels, as well as other factors like pedestrian and vehicular traffic. Additionally, they can be remotely monitored and controlled using IoT technology.We have introduced smart street lights that save power by in corporating motion sensors to recognize objects and dynam ically adjust the light intensity. If a passing object is heavier, such as a car, the light intensity increases, whereas for lighter objects like a dog, the brightness is reduced. Furthermore, we have integrated an ESP8266 processor to process input, analyze object size, and control the brightness of 5V LEDs accordingly. This approach optimizes energy consumption, enhances efficiency, and ensures effective street lighting management.IoT technology allows smart street light sys tems to gather data and feedback on energy consumption, maintenance needs, and other important factors. This data can then be used to optimize the system, improve energy efficiency, and reduce costs, making it a vital component of modern urban infrastructure [1,2].
