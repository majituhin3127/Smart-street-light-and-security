Smart Street Light and Women Security

In the era of smart cities, our Smart Street Light System uses automation, energy-efficient LED lighting, and real-time IoT monitoring to enhance urban safety, reduce costs, and promote sustainability.

Introduction

Smart street lights use sensors and ESP8266 controllers to adjust brightness dynamically. They help save energy by dimming lights when detecting smaller objects and brightening for larger ones. These systems can be remotely managed using IoT.

Decision Making Algorithm

Lights turn on when motion is detected and adjust brightness based on object size and light conditions to optimize energy use.

Energy Saving Strategy

Using adaptive light control and motion-based brightness ensures efficient energy usage and future integration with renewable energy.
